Our Mission
DeckBridge is a student-led initiative that creates educational trading cards, intending to introduce young children to content not taught in the present school curricula. We believe education refines the lens through which we see the world, something particularly valuable for children. Thus, we aim to transform passive learning into what children enjoy - something accessible, engaging and explorable.
