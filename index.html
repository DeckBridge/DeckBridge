The EEE Aproach

E - Explore

We support children's exploration in traditionally "trivial" subjects, empowering their connection to the real world and the exposure to diverse hobby or career.

E - Engage

We make learning engaging and encourage active curiosity. By trading and collecting cards, children not only learn, but also socialize simultaneously.

E - Expand

We connect with our community by partnering with local organizations for free distribution of our cards. Our diverse team ensures that each community is served by people who already belong to it.
